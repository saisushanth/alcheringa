Organiser's desk

Dear Prospective Delegates,

It gives me immense pleasure to present to you, the maiden edition of IITG Model United Nations Conference, as a part of our annual cultural festival Alcheringa, to be held from 30th January to 2nd February 2014.

“We can’t always build the future for our youth, but we can build our youth for the future.”
With this vision in mind, we provide you with a platform to deliberate and discuss pressing global issues, while you develop yourself as a rounded and conscious individual with a powerful skill-set to lead the World in future. The IITG MUN will put you in the shoes of a leader, and test your powers of persuasion, presence of mind, commitment among many other qualities.

We are hosting a General Assembly- Disarmament and International Security Committee (DISEC), which will witness fierce debate on the responsibility for nations to intervene for humanitarian purposes, and the question of authority to do so. Upholding our high academic standards, we promise to deliver an excellent quality of discussion and organization, an experience you will never forget. And while you contend with fellow participants from around the country, your thoughts and position on the issues would be cross-examined by a highly punctilious team of International Press.

And as long as you will be here, you also get a golden opportunity to witness eminent personalities from all over the world perform Live, as a part of Alcheringa. We will put in our best efforts to make sure your stay is a memorable and growing experience. We all here look forward to meet you this February.

Karan Rajwanshi

Secretary General,
IITG Model United Nations Conference


What is IITG MUN

Born with the aim to bring out the best in every individual, the concept of IITG MUN is guided by a set of values and goals that seeks to provide every individual “a hands down” idea of the intricacies of the decision making process at international level, in an effort to provide holistic development of society as a unit. We have placed our firm belief on the idea of bringing together people from all over India, separated by regions, principles and cultures, united by humanity, to be a part of this platform and facilitate meaningful dialogue and discussion on wide ranging international issues, which affect the international community as a whole.
With a vision to witness one of the most advanced, intense and crucial debate in the committees of IITG MUN, we strive to imbibe the virtues of leadership, co-operation, negotiations and diplomatic competence, and not only serve as a pathway to self-development, but also to nurture the policy makers of future generation, who will ultimately bring end to the misery and misfortunes of the world, through novel ideas and suggestions. We seek to break free from all the tangled threads of predefined notions and ideas, to engage in an enhancing debate and redefine all the boundaries and ideas, to provide all the students involved, from the delegates, the Executive Board, and the Secretariat; an idea of conflict resolution through peaceful means. At IITG MUN, you will definitely get the best you can, from the perfectly planned agendas, accomplished executive board, to most comprehensive delegate guides; to garner maximum participation of the delegates. We are at constant efforts to outdo ourselves to provide our delegates the best possible environment to make the MUN one of the most renowned one’s throughout India.
