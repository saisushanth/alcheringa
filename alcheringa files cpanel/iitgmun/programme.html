EVENT

Eloquence, exuberance, enthrallment. IIT Guwahati Model United Nations or IITG MUN as we like to call it , promises to be all that and much more! With its inaugural edition of Model United Nations ,in probably its most awaited edition, LitSoc IIT Guwahati and Alcheringa are trying to bring out a unique and fresh approach to MUN debating.

Our philosophy is to approach the MUN debates in a ground breaking way. Be prepared to face adversity in the face of prospective alliances and to find unlikely allies in supposed enemies. No more will pre-knowledge suffice to fuel your way to glory. No longer will the set standards guide you to recognitions. Be prepared to unleash your imagination and break all bounds. Let your instincts guide you, may your sheer wits and intellect alone determine where you’ll make your stand, The schedule of events will be announced soon.
