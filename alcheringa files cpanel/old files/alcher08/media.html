Revel in Retro with Alcheringa’08 @ IIT Guwahati

"All the world's a stage, and all the men and women merely players", said Shakespeare. Well, Alcheringa says Come and Play! Come and Play! The stage is set and it's your turn to set it on fire. Yes, IIT-Guwahati's much awaited cultural festival is coming your way again and it promises to more fun than you can fill the Brahmaputra with. With a mind-boggling array of events, huge Pro-Shows for headliners and loads and loads of fun n' masti, it would be crime to contemplate going anywhere other than IIT Guwahati's picturesque campus between Jan 31st and Feb 3rd. And what’s more, this year’s Alcheringa, like all the previous editions comes with a cause: AIDS and the Gangetic Dolphin.
While the fest retains its reputation for high-quality entertainment, the event’s two main attractions will be the pro-nites: Crescendo and Juggernaut. The mega-event which has earlier played host to crowds in excess of 10,000! Will be bigger than ever before on February 3rd.  On February 2nd, for all the headbangers is the Juggernaut featuring to the likes of Parikrama, Motherjane, Vayu. And on February 1st is the classical nite Saaz.
Like every year, this year too a common theme runs through the soul of the event: Retro. Given our generation’s fascination with the lifestyle of the 70’s, this theme seems only too appropriate. Pop culture has become too popular and Alcheringa ’08 provides visitors a fun opportunity to be transported back in time to the carefree era of the Flower Children and forget their tension for a day as they revel in retro. While Elvis won’t actually be there on stage, every attempt has been made to recreate the era, with almost all events staging a retro round and retro being incorporated into the smallest of details. But to see the real thing, one will just have to be there!
As far as the events go, Alcheringa offers avenues for people of any and every talent to express themselves. Those who can’t stand still when the music starts have an array of three different events to choose from and the same goes for those who can’t help but break into a song when the moment arrives. As for those who shy away from naach-gaana and the pen is their best friend, the literary events have been planned just for them. And then the lucky lot who has the gift of wit or instinct for entertainment, well, Alcher will most certainly not disappoint. From the innovative game shows to many other offstage games, good-natured fun is the very soul of the festival. Not to forget the events rightfully called Alcher’s Exclusive: Hot Couture, the fashion show and one of the definitive rock competitions of the region: Rock-o-Phonix. This event is living testament to the fact that rock is the lifeblood of the North-East… past winners have gone on to win nationwide recognition. Expect to see smashing performances this year as well.
And what’s more, you can become the charming dancer or become the entertainer extraordinaire at Alcher with the Dance and RJ workshops we have lined up for you.
So don’t miss all this and more. All you have to do is get an Alcheringa pass from your college’s General Secretary or contact the organizers (all details are put up on www.alchering.org) and bring along some ID. It’s as simple as that.
As one can very well fathom, Alcheringa in itself is a once-in-a-lifetime opportunity to lose yourself amidst throngs of like-minded people as one either watches India’s leading playback singer perform in front of your eyes or head bangs to the thunderous music of soul-gratifying rock music, as one is mesmerized by the crystal clear notes of a classical singer or the liquid moves of an accomplished dancer. Alcheringa has something for everyone, and all this- the dream of a few dedicated students.
