Registration Rules

  1. Please notify us 5 days in advance of your scheduled arrival such that we can arrange for your travel to the campus.
  2. Faculty will be accommodated in separate rooms.
  3. All participants are required to carry their college identity cards and bonafide Certificates.
  4. Consumption of alcoholic drinks and/or narcotic drugs in the campus is strictly prohibited. Participants shall not enter the campus in intoxicated state and should not possess such materials. IIT Guwahati is strictly a no smoking zone.
  5. Any form of obscene or unacceptable behavior or language on or off stage is strictly prohibited and will lead to immediate disqualification of the college.
  6. The team should not damage any infrastructure while in IITG campus.
  7. Organizers decision is final in case of any complaints.
  8. Any complaints regarding judges’ decisions will not be entertained.
  9. Organizers reserve the right to change any of the rules mentioned above.
