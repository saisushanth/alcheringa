Terms and conditions

We at Alcheringa, strive to make your short stay with us, as pleasant and comfortable as we can. You will have to register yourself at Hospitality Desk upon arrival in IITG campus. Allotments of accommodation, mess facilities are some of the functions of Hospitality Desk. Whether you are worried about your accommodation, food or transport, wondering what the events are all about, confused about the schedule or worried about your registrations, we will be at your service 24 hours a day.

  • Hospitality Control Desk will be setup in KAMENG HOSTEL for BOYS. Directions to reach Hospitality Control Desk will be there inside the campus.
  • Girls should reach SUBANSIRI hostel, where hospitality control desk will be setup.
  • Production of ID card is mandatory at the Hospitality Desk.
  • Alcheringa’08 ID cards will be issued to each team member after registration and production of ID card will be compulsory for taking part in any event.
  • Volunteers will be there to guide you from hospitality control desks to your accommodation.

Accommodation

  • All accommodation arrangements will be done in various hostels of IITG for which you will have to pay a sum of Rs.100 per person which will cover lodging for 5 days.
  • We suggest that you carry a lock for your own safety, and a mosquito repellant to make your nights more comfortable.
  • Please note that the accommodation is available only for a period of 6 days, that is from 30th of January to 4th of February. Participants are requested to vacate by 4th February.
  • Accommodation will be strictly on first-come-first-serve basis.

    • Food              

    Food arrangements will be done in hostel mess where you will be staying, which will cost you around Rs. 50 a day. Alternatively you can eat from food stalls at the venue or from canteens in hostel itself.
