Dear alumnus,

One fine morning, while beginning your day, have you been struck by the thought that some years back your sun would rise majestically some where in an eastern corner of India , where every moment of the rest of your day shall be spent with some most wonderful people called friends who'll see you thru the boring lectures of profs, thru bora canteen, thru the guitar of the next door bhaand, thru the dooms days of midsems n endsems, thru the poetic ferry crossing over Brahmaputra, thru bday parties at JB's, thru deafening roars of Alcheringa, thru wild hooting of manthan…………..the list of these and many more unforgettable moments, so very indelibly imprinted on your mind and heart alike, is indeed endless.

Although Guwahati has changes, the provinces where you used to have all the masti has undergone drastic changes, but not are the feelings, the respect, the memories of the fun moments we had with you. And we know nor has the changed the attachments you had for IIT Guwahati.

Warmest greetings of the new year from IIT Guwahati. We hope the love spread across the message finds u in the same indefatigable spirit IITG always found you here.

As you know, there has never been a dull moment in IITG. Life here is always buzzing with activities. And this time around , it is our beloved Alcheringa that is keeping us busy.

We take the pleasure – genuine pleasure, not what we write in the sponsorship brochures – in inviting YOU to Alcheringa 2008.

"An IITG person delivers what he promises, and he promises what he can deliver."

With these words of Dr. Barua, you people left this place. With this spirit itself, we promise to you, an Alcheringa in a never seen before color. Yes!! You must be glad to learn that Alcher– the Diwali of the iitgian calendar-- is turning 12 this year. With the pinch of retro style this year Alcheringa aims at the resurrection of the lost retro look giving the festival its own unique feeling. Remember those looks which used to be a fashion statement at one time ……generations have passed by and so has the fashion……but its revival in the youth will burn out the sensational feeling inside you. Your presence on its 12th birthday here shall certainly instill in us new confidence and enthusiasm to take Alcheringa to the heights expected by the vision with which Alcheringa was started.

The "alumni meet" organized on the sidelines of Alcheringa-2007(last year) saw unexpected number of alumni swarming the campus and recreating the old magic --to our utmost pleasure filling the air with some nostalgia. Alcheringa this year warms to receiving even greater response from your side. The never ending fun and euphoria that we have during this festive period of Alcher shall be even more in your company, it'll really be icing on the cake. This Alcheringa is gonna be no less than a carnival and we are sure that u shall stay mesmerized in its beautiful memories even after Alcher 08 bids us farewell. And who knows, you may get to see again your love that you may have found in some beauty from the city, who u might have been talking to, till now, only on phone or thru mails. Alcher any way does bring a good number of bombshells here in the campus, so you may find a new love altogether.

The dates of Alcheringa 2008 have been decided as 31st January, 1st,2nd, and 3rd February. At the cost of sounding formal we sincerely extend our cordial invitation to you and request you to take time off your busy schedules and visit ye old Alma mater. C'mon people you gotcha make that one trip to the place where you spent four most beautiful years of your life .We sincerely hope you will provide us with an opportunity to meet, talk, in a word, experience the first people who were brave and intrepid (and pretty much reckless) enough to be IITians from Guwahati. . We are always at your service if you need any kind of information.

Waiting for you with folded hands and an open heart.So what are you waiting for, get your tickets booked and your bags packed.

Thanking you
Sincerely
Organizing Team
