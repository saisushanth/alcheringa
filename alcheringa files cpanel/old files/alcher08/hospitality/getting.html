How to reach IIT Guwahati campus??

From Railway Station

  • Transportation to IITG campus will be provided to all registered participants.
  • A stall would be set up at the railway station by Alcheringa’08 hospitality team.
  • Volunteers may be found on the platform, near the main entrance gate, carrying Alcheringa banners.

  In case you are not able to spot any of the Alcheringa volunteers,
you have following options:

  • Shuttle buses (white n green carrying IITG placard), provided by institute, will ply from the city almost every hour and will operate from Pan Bazar Water Tank, a stone’s throw from the railway station.
  • Auto rickshaws (@Rs.150-175) or Sumos (@Rs.300-350) can also be hired. Ferry service can also be availed from Fancy Bazaar.

From Airport

    • You can: Hire a taxi to IIT Guwahati. Inside the campus, get down at the Hospitality Desk (Directions to reach Control Room will be there inside the campus).

    ::NOTE::            

    • Picking up of participants is subject to availability of transportation facility
    • The number of candidates requiring transportation facility should exceed a minimum of 10.
    • Exceptions may be made in special cases.

    Participants need to inform us at least a week in advance by emailing us at publicity_alcheringa@iitg.ernet.in. Confirmations will be emailed to you.
