2-Steps – Couple Dance with a Twist

Rules for prelims:

  • No. of couples per team should not be more than 4.
  • The team has to perform on a self chosen song.
  • First for a sequence of song, Boys and girls will be dancing like the boys themselves and girls themselves respectively.
  • Then they will have to swap the role that is boys doing the female steps and girls doing male steps for the same sequence to be repeated again.
  • The same sequence of song should be recorded twice in the CD with an interval of 5-10 seconds which will be allotted for role reversal.
  • Total duration will be 2 minutes.
  • The performance done in the preliminary round can also be repeated in the finals.

Rules for Finals

  • There are three rounds in the finals:
    1. 1st round- (Retro+Prop)
      • In this round, the participants have to dance on a song of their choice.
      • The song chosen should depict the Retro era i.e. the song should not be later than 80’s.
      • The participants have to use a Prop in this round, which will contain some points in the total marks.
      • Maximum duration will be 3 minutes
    2. 2nd round- (Role swapping on a buzzer)
      • A team will perform on the song selected by them before hand.
      • Boys and girls will be dancing like the boys themselves and girls themselves respectively and they will have to swap the roles on a buzzer that is boys doing the female steps and girls doing male steps.
      • The buzzer will be rung by the organizers/judges at any random time.
      • Maximum duration of this round will be 4 minutes.
    3. 3rd round- (Role copying)
      • The team has to choose a sequence of song of their choice.
      • First for that sequence of song, Boys and girls will be dancing like the boys themselves and girls themselves respectively.

      Note:

      • The songs in all the 3 rounds should be different.
      • Creativity and innovativeness will fetch extra marks
      • Props can be used in any round.
      • Props will include anything which is moved from its initial position during the performance. It can be a part of clothing.
      • Cds and cassettes should be submitted to the organizers at least 30 minutes before the start of the event. No team is allowed to bring its own CPUs. The audio format should be compatible with Windows Media Player or Winamp(preferably mp3 format). A duplicate copy of the same should be maintained.
      • Negative marks will be awarded for exceeding the time limit.
      • One member from the team should sit with the sound technician and computer operator.
      • Clearing of the stage is the responsibility of the team.
      • Any prerecorded or live music may be used during the show.
      • The maximum time means the time taken from stage-in to stage-out

      Judging criteria:

      1. 1st round:
        • Performance 50%
        • Prop Usage 20%
        • Choice of song 15%
        • Costumes 15%
      2. 2nd round:
        • Performance 50%
        • Role reversal 30%
        • Spontaneity 20%
      3. 3rd round:
        • Performance 50%
        • Copying 50%
