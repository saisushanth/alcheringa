Shades – Fine Arts Event

Teen too - Body Art

Ever imagined yourself as a punk having a funky tattoo all over your body? Take part in this event and find out what it feels like.

General Guidelines:

  • There will be maximum 3 participants per team including the model.
  • Water colors will be provided by organizers.
  • Participants can get their own sparkles or mehendi if they prefer to.
  • Face, neck, shoulders, belly, hands, back and legs can be painted.
  • Time allotted is two hrs.
  • Theme would be given on the spot.

My Style- T-shirt making

Haven’t you always felt that you could have designed a better T-shirt than the one you bought? Prove it here!
 

General Guidelines:

  • Each team has to design a T-shirt.
  • A white T-shirt, colors, scissors and needle-thread will be provided by the organizers. Apart from the usual painting, you can also cut and stitch the T-shirt to showcase your innovation.
  • The team can have a maximum of 2 members.
  • Topic will be given on the spot.
  • Time allotted is 3 hrs.

Illustrative Collage

  General Guidelines:  
  • A pile of news papers and magazines along with a white chart paper, markers, fevicol, cello-tape and scissors will be provided to each team.
  • The team can have a maximum of 3 members.
  • A topic will be given and the team has to cut the items related to it and paste them on a chart paper by arranging them to get a clear picture of the theme.
  • Time allotted is 3 hrs.

Graffiti- Street painting

  General Guidelines:  
  • Each team should paint a given area of street.
  • The maximum number of participants per team is 4.
  • A preliminary round will be conducted and the top 5 teams will go through to the finals.
  • paints, brushes and chalk will be provided.
  • Theme will be given on the spot.
  • Time allotted is 4 hrs.

Zig Zag - Hair Styling

  General Guidelines:  
  • Combs, hair colours and Gel will be provided by the organizers.
  • Any extra tool like hair-pins, beads, etc. can be brought by the participants as well.
  • A wig can not be used.
  • The theme of hair styling will be retro
  • The maximum number of participants per team is 3.

Crazy canvas- the modern art contest

  General Guidelines:  
  • You will be provided with scissors, fevicol, tape and a thermocol sheet.
  • Any kind of material like leaves, twigs, pebbles, clay etc can be used to create an art piece on a thermocol sheet. These will not be provided by the organizers.
  • NO readymade colouring materials like crayons, pencils, paints should be used.
  • Time allotted is 4 hrs.
