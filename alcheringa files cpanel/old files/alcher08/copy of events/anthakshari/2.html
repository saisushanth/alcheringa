Antakshari – Gaane Anjaane

Rules:

Preliminary round:

  1. Each team must consist of 2 members.
  2. Preliminary round will comprise of two tests namely the knowledge & the vocal ability test. Knowledge Test: Examination will consist of 30 objective & 10 subjective questions with unique answers. Time provided will be one hour. Vocal Ability Test: This will be a test for singing where the participants are asked to sing a song, amongst a list of 20, given by the organizers & decision will be based entirely upon the choice of the judges.
  3. The language of the songs will be Hindi.

Finals:

  1. Finally 4 teams will be selected from the preliminary round for the final onstage event.
  2. The duration will be of around 1 hour 30 minutes & the event will consist of 6-7 rounds.
  3. Questions will be based on movie and album songs and audiovisuals.
  4. Participants must have fair idea of particulars of popular songs.
  5. Some of the rounds will hold negative marking & relative marking in accordance with the quality of the question to be asked.
  6. Bonus rounds will also be there.
