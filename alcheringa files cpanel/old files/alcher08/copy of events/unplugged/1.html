Unplugged – Vocal Contest

This is a singing contest. Nothing more. Nothing less.

Unplugged is where you are on your own. We board up all the electrical sockets. Maybe the gentle rhythm of an acoustic guitar or the soft beat of bongo drums… that’s all.

First you show us that what you can do with a song of you own choice; and then you’ll take a trip back to the melodious yesteryear. Yes, you’ll have to sing RETRO.
