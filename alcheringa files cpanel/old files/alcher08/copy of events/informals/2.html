Alcher Jam – Solo Impromptu Dance competition

General Guidelines:

  1. Participants should tap their feet to the songs played by the organizers only in the competition.
  2. Each participant will be given an around 2 min slot to perform and no second chance will be given.
  3. All the participants will be divided in the group of 5 randomly and only one from the each group will move to the finals.
  4. The registration forms will be available at the control desk on the first and second day of the fest and registrations go till the scheduled time of event.
  5. Registrations are limited and will be informed at the control desk.
  6. Organizers decision will be final and binding.
  7. Age limit for the participation would be 15 or above.
  8. No Props are allowed.
