Electric Heels – The Choreo Showdown

“Thunder is good, thunder is impressive, but it is lightning that does the work”

No Introduction required!!
The famous Electric Heels stage will be set ablaze by the lightning unleashed as India’s best choreographers’ feet come down with all the energy of a million-volt bolt.

The rounds are impressive!
Dazzle in the darkness in the UV Round, while the Retro round gives you a chance to either gyrate like Shammi Kapoor or do the moonwalk like Michael Jackson.
And to express yourself with total freedom in the Open Round and send a message to everyone in the Theme Round.
