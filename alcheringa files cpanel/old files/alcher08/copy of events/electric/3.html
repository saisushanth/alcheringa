Electric Heels – The Choreo Showdown

Prelims

  • In prelims you are required to present your “conventional round "(refer to finals) choreography.

Finals
The final consists of four rounds:

  1. Conventional
    • You are required to perform a dance of your own choice.
    • This round will be of maximum 6 minutes and a minimum of 4 minutes
  2. Retro round
    • You have to perform a dance in retro style on a song chosen from the 60s 70s or 80s. Your choreography, costumes, expressions and props should create a feel of that classic era.
    • This round will be of maximum 4 minutes and minimum 3 minutes.
  3. Ultraviolet round
    • This round will take place in the UV lights. Rest all lights will be switched off.
    • The property of the UV lights is that it makes white colour glow in the dark. Nothing except the white color is visible in the dark with UV lights on.
    • This round will be of maximum 4 minutes and minimum 3 minutes.
  4. The Topikos (Theme round)
    • Your performance must aim at creating awareness to prevailing social problems. Your theme can be for e.g. AIDS, DOWRY etc.
    • Theme selection carries points.
    • This round will be of maximum 4 minutes and minimum 3 minutes.
