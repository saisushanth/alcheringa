Kirnaankan – Photography and Filming

Digital Photography

  • General Rules & Guidelines
    • Photographs must be taken from the campus only.
    • Photographs must be digital.
    • Size of the pictures should not be less than 2048*1536 pixels; this is close to 3.2 mega pixels.
    • Theme for photograph will be given by 1000 hours, on 31st Jan, 2008.
    • 7 pictures including 2 black and white on the given theme have to be submitted at the control desk positively by 1800 hours, on 2nd Feb, 2007
  • Editing or correction
    • Editing of images is allowed to an extent as to correct colors and change contrast.
    • Addition of foreign elements into the pictures will lead to disqualification. You are not supposed to do copy and paste and then put multiple elements in the image.
    • Image should look natural and realistic.
    • Editing is just allowed to improve the quality of the picture, not to transform the overall meaning of the picture.
    • You are supposed to submit the original picture also along with the edited picture.
  • Judging Criteria

              Each photo will be judged separately in the following manner:
    • Portrayal of theme - how well and logical your photograph is in accordance with the theme given. 30%
    • Composition - how well the elements are placed in your photograph. Relation in the elements can be of following form: rhythm, harmony, contrast and balance. 30%
    • Skill - how well you have taken a photograph in terms of skill. For ex: focus, lighting condition. - 20%
    • Innovation - How innovative you are in taking the photographs 20%.
    Note: The 5 colour photos will receive 75% weightage and the 2 Black and white
     photos will receive 25% weightage.

    Online Photography:

  • General Rules & Guidelines
    • Photographs must be digital.
    • Size of the pictures should not be less than 2048*1536 pixels; this is close to 3.2 mega pixels.
    • A theme shall be announced every 14 days. Photographs must be submitted within 13 days of the announcement of the theme. Each competition over 2 weeks will be independent of the others and prizes shall also be separate. The 1st theme shall be announced as soon as the website is launched.
    • All photographs must be accompanied by a small caption.
    • Please refrain from taking photographs where it is prohibited to do so. Alcheringa will not be liable for the same. Also please do not submit copyrighted photos.
    • Photos must have been taken after the announcement of the theme.
  • Editing or correction
    • As in Digital Photography
  • Judging Criteria

              Each photo will be judged separately in the following manner:
    • Portrayal of theme - how well and logical your photograph is in accordance with the theme given. 25%
    • Composition - how well the elements are placed in your photograph. Relation in the elements can be of following form: rhythm, harmony, contrast and balance. 25%
    • Skill - how well you have taken a photograph in terms of skill. For ex: focus, lighting condition. - 15%
    • Innovation - How innovative you are in taking the photographs 20%.
    • Caption: How good and appropriate the caption for each photo is with respect to the photograph and the theme.20%
  • Submissions

    • You have to submit 5 photographs.
    • Photographs must be mailed to sansiddha@iitg.ernet.in and a carbon copy must be sent to k.chandan@iitg.ernet.in . Your photos shall be uploaded on the Alcheringa website.

    Movie Making

  • General Rules & Guidelines
    • The Movie must be digital in format.
    • The movie will have to be shot inside the IIT campus. You will have to cover one of the events of Alcheringa which shall be specified beforehand.
    • The movie must be of at least 320x240 in size and maximum 15 minutes in duration. You can shoot with a digital video camera or digital camera which supports movies.
  • Editing or correction
    • No editing is allowed.
  • Judging Criteria
    • Skill: Your skill in shooting the video shall be judged. 25%
    • Portrayal: How well you have been able to portray what has to be shown.45%
    • Composition: How well the frame of the video is composed.30%

    Common instructions for all Kirnaankan events:

    • All photographs will be the property of Alcheringa.
    • Judges decision will be final and mandatory.
