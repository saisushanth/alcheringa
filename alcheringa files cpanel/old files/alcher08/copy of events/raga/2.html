Raga High – The Indian Music Contest

General Guidelines:

  1. The event will first consist of prelims followed by finals.
  2. Bands will be given 15 minutes (Stage in to stage out) for the prelims and 25 minutes for the finals.
  3. One original composition is required to be performed in both the prelims and the finals.
  4. Each band must perform 4 songs in the finals. You can perform more if you want and time permits.
  5. The 4 songs of finals should fall into the following categories -:
    • 1 Original Composition. (No remixes or parodies allowed.)
    • 1 Jingle of minimum 30 seconds, the topic of which will be given 1 day before the event.
    • 1 as per Judges Choice.(From the list of 3 songs submitted by the band)
    • 1 Free Song i.e. song of your own choice and it can be solo or group.
  6. A list of 3 probable songs must be provided to the organizers before the competition
  7. A band must consist of at least one vocalist and 2 instrumentalists.
  8. One person can represent one band only.
  9. The language of the songs should be strictly Hindi.
  10. Lyrics may be referred to during the performance.
  11. Negative points for exceeding the time limit.
  12. Any Additional Requirement for the performance should be informed earlier.
