Gold Rush – The Treasure Hunt

General Guidelines:

  1. This event will run on each day of Alcheringa
  2. Registration may be done only in a couple comprising a male and a female.
  3. It would be helpful in forming couple if you mail your profile which must include
    • A full size colored photograph.
    • Your name, age, sex, height, qualification and hobbies & interests.
    • The reason “why should one choose you as partner?” (Not more than 30 words).
  4. You have to send your profile latest by 1800 hours, 31st Jan.
  5. In case if you are not able to send your profile, we will be helping you to make the couple.
  6. You will have the choice to propose anyone from the profile or accepting the proposals made to you.
  7. Same couple will be collecting the clues during the whole day.

You can send your profile on these Emails and for further query you can contact on given nos. in contacts.
