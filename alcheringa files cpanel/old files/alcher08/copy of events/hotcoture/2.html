Hot Couture – The Fashion Contest

General Guidelines:

  1. Competition is open for college students (including fashion institutes). All participants have to submit photocopy of college identity card or a permission letter from head of the institute.
  2. Minimum number of models per team is 6.
  3. Teams can take help from professional designers, choreographers, and makeup artists. Only 1 choreographer, make-up artisi and designer will be accepted from one team. But these professionals will not be considered as a part of team and will not be eligible for the BEST CHOREOGRAPHER, the BEST MAKEUP ARTIST and the BEST DESIGNER.
  4. Choreographers, makeup artists, designers can also be ramp models.
  5. Each team is required to submit 3 photographs of each model online or along with registration form, along with the name of the model.
  6. Every team will be given 5 minutes for each round (stage-in to stage-out).

    7. On exceeding the time limit, negative points will be awarded.
  7. The teams are responsible for their own music and sequence
  8. Music is to be submitted in CD in a format compatible with Windows media player(preferably in mp3 format), which must contain all the music (for prelims round and the finals) with properly named files and folders. The teams are expected to label the CDs properly and also name the music files according to the sequences to avoid confusion. All CDs must be submitted to the organizers at least 1 hour before start of the prelims.
  9. One team member should be present near the computer to help the organizers in playing the music smoothly.
  10. Lighting facility is to be availed. One person of the group can supervise else it will be up to the lights group.
  11. Water, Fire and animals in any form will not be allowed on the stage and the ramp.
  12. Vulgarity in any form will not be allowed in the competition. So if the team feels any stunt could be termed as vulgar, it would be best to speak of it to the organizer before performing it on the stage.
  13. Prelims will be held on the second day of Alcheringa, 1st February 2008. Each team has to report at least one hour before the prelims. The Final is scheduled for 3rd February 2008.
  14. All participants must register online on our website www.alcheringa.org. Registration will start from 10th November, 2008 and closes one week before event. No last moment on the spot entries will be allowed.
  15. All outstation participants are requested to fill online hospitality form before arriving here.
  16. Organizers reserve the right to cancel any round and disqualify teams which do not adhere to the rules.
  17. The decision of the judge will be considered to be the final one.
  18. Rules might undergo slight changes depending on the situation. Please refer to the website www.alcheringa.org for the updated rules.

JUDGING CRITERIA:
· Costumes(Creativity, relevance to the round and the theme)
· Choreography
· Ramp Walk
· Stage Coverage
· Coordination
· Music
· Originality and Innovation
