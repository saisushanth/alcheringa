Hot Couture – The Fashion Contest

Ever dreamt of becoming a Fashion Designer or a Supermodel? Hot Couture is where you can showcase your creations. If you clear the highly competitive prelims you’ll be star of the show in three very different rounds.
In the Wacky Round, you’ll have to show the one of the largest crowds of Alcher what your idea of fashion is.

In the Traditional Round, you’ll have to showcase the apparel of any culture.

And finally, in the essence of Alcher ’08, you’ll have to go Retro.

So let’s hear the slice of the fabric, the music as the models do their elegant catwalk. Let’s see colours of the design. Let’s see Hot Couture.
