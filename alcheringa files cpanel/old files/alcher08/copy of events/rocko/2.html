Rock-o-Phonix – The Rock Contest

General Guidelines:

  1. The competition is in 2 stages. Best 5 teams from prelims will compete in the finals.
  2. Teams will be given 20 minutes (Stage in to stage out) for the prelims and 30 minutes for the finals.
  3. A maximum of 2 songs can be repeated from your prelims performance play-list in the finals.
  4. One original composition is required to be performed in both the prelims and the finals. The best original composition will be awarded. The original can be of any regional language also.
  5. The teams will be required to perform one 30 second jingle in the finals, theme of which will be given on the same day. The lyrics of the jingle have to be provided by the teams before the show.
  6. Teams should report at least one hour before the competition

    7.
  7. A list of 10 probable songs, out which there must be minimum three Original compositions, must be provided before the competition.
  8. The first song of your performance (prelims) will be of the judges’ choice.
  9. The teams are required to send a demo of their original composition to Rockophonix@gmail.com with the Subject as “ROPDEMOS_<Band Name>_<Name of OC>”
  10. A maximum of 15 teams will be selected for the prelims based on their Original composition and their team profile.
  11. Explicit or offensive lyrics/gestures guarantee immediate expulsion from the stage.
  12. Booze/Drugs/Smoke strictly disallowed on campus.
  13. Judges’ and organizers’ decision will be final and binding.
  14. The rules are subject to change without any prior notice.
  15. Organizers reserve the right to admission.
  16. Outstation bands will be provided accommodation for Rs. 100.
  17. Bands are encouraged to bring their own gear/add-ons. Only drum kit and sound arrangements will be provided by the organizers.
  18. Bands that make to top five will be given one way transportation charges (second class sleeper equivalent in case of train) on the production of the bus or train tickets.
