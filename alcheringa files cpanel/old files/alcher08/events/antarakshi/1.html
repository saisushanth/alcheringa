Antakshari – Gaane Anjaane

“Baite baite kya karen? Karna hai kucch kaam…
Shuru karo Antakshari… Leke Prabhu ka naam.”

Reminded of those long nights spent with friends, when life mostly consisted of thinking up songs to be used in tomorrow’s game? Of the days when every song, both new and old, were at the tip of your tongue?
Well, here is where you’ll relive those good old days. No iPods, no walkmans… it’s just you, your teammate and the years spent listening to music.

“Prabhu ka naam… Ma… shuru karo!!”
