Crazy Tune – Best in 60 seconds

General Guidelines:

  1. The music can be produced by any instrument.
  2. The organisers will provide instruments like matki, table as drums, Electric guitar, acoustic guitar, flute, casio etc.
  3. You can also bring your own instrument.
  4. The time limit is 60 seconds.
  5. Only 1 participant is allowed at a time.
  6. Same participant can play different instruments again, but not the same one.
  7. The musical instrument can be of any type, except any pre-recorded form.
