Unplugged – Vocal Contest

Rules:

  1. Maximum 2 members in each group are allowed.
  2. Any kind of pre recorded music will not be allowed.
  3. Musical instrument can only consist of non-electrical instruments
    Like acoustic guitar, Congo, harmonium, flute, violin etc.
  4. The language should only be English or Hindi.
  5. Judging will not be on the music but only on the vocals.
  6. The time limit for the song is 3 minutes.

Prelims:

  1. A song of participant’s choice has to be performed.

Mains:

  1. It will consist of 2 rounds.
  2. Song in the 1st round will be of participant’s choice.
  3. 2nd round will be the RETRO round. The song should not be later than 80’s.
  4. The time limit for each song is 3 minutes.
