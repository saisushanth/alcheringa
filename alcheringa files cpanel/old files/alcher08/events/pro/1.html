Shuruat-e-Alcher  

This is the curtain-raiser to the huge… no, wait, GRAND opening to the North-East region’s biggest Cultural Festival. All the hype surrounding Alcher will be fully justified on this Opening Nite; a vibrant amalgamation of numerous performances by well-known artistes.

In the past, audiences have witnessed such auditory and visual delights like a Mime Show by nationally acclaimed artise Moinul Haq, a Jazz Show by Prasanna, Stunning Dance Performances and many more.

Shuruaat-e-Alcher is the gateway to a plethora of amazing events which comprise Alcheringa 2008… be there!
