Bollywood Calling – The Gag show

General Guidelines:

  1. Minimum age of participants must be 15 years.
  2. Languages used should be either Hindi/English or both.
  3. Any kind of vulgarity would lead to disqualification.
  4. The team members should report to the organizer half an hour before the commencement of the event.

Prelims (100 points):

  • Team has to perform spoof and all its rules and guidelines are same as mentioned below in the spoof round of the finals.
  • The performance done in prelims can also be done in the finals.

    • Finals Rounds:

    Sayest which thou don’t see (FILL THE DIALOGUES) (100 points)
    1. The teams have to select their own movie clip beforehand which will be projected on the screen.
    2. The dialogues of the original scene will be Mute. The participants are supposed to fill them with their dialogues on the stage.
    3. The teams can keep the music in the background, if they wish.
    4. Minimum number of participants must be 2.
    5. Maximum time limit is 4 minutes (curtain to curtain). Minimum is 2 min. Exceeding time limit will lead to negative points.
    6. Delivery of the same dialogues from the clip will lead to reduction of points.
    7. It would be preferable if a famous clip is chosen for the parody)

    Judging criteria

    • Dialogues and dialogue delivery i.e., how well you are able to create humour-70%
    • Scene synching with the dialogues- 30%

  • As you like it ( SPOOF )(100 points)
    • The team has to act a parody.
      Parody- humorous imitation of an author work, act, style, incident etc.
    • Minimum number of participants is 4.
    • Maximum time limit is 6 min (curtain to curtain). Minimum is 3 min. Exceeding time limit will lead to penalty.


      • Judging criteria
        1. Performance and acting- 50%
        2. Presentation encompassing costumes, music, props, etc.- 30%
        3. Innovation- 20%

    • The Big fight ( SITUATIONAL SPOOF )(150 points):

      • Teams have to play a spoof on the given situations.
        Topics of the situation will be uploaded on the website 3 weeks before the event.
        Maximum time limit is 6 min (curtain to curtain). Minimum is 4 min. Exceeding time limit will lead to penalty.
        Minimum number of participants is 3.

      • Judging criteria
        Costumes, music and props- 15%
        Performance and acting- 55%
        Innovation- 30%
      • Penalty Rules:
        Exceeding time will result in penalty
        First 10 seconds - - 3% of total points of event.
        For every next 10 seconds -- 5% of total points of event
