Electric Heels

General Guidelines:

  1. Total number of members in a team should be at least 3 and at most 25.
  2. Any team not ready by the specified time will be disqualified.
  3. Lighting facility is to be availed. One person of the group can supervise else it will be up to the lights group.
  4. Creativity and innovativeness will fetch extra marks.
  5. Props can be used in any round.
  6. Props will include anything which is moved from its initial position during the performance. It can be a part of clothing.
  7. Cds and cassettes should be submitted to the organizers at least 30 minutes before the start of the event. No team is allowed to bring its own CPUs. The audio format should be compatible with Windows Media Player or Winamp(preferably mp3 format). A duplicate copy of the same should be maintained.
  8. Negative marks will be awarded for exceeding the time limit.
  9. One member from the team should sit with the sound technician and computer operator.
  10. Clearing of the stage is the responsibility of the team.
  11. Any prerecorded or live music may be used during the show.
  12. The maximum time means the time taken from stage-in to stage-out

JUDGING CRITERIA:
· Precision: Are the dancers on beat?
· Synchronization/Coherence: Is the group dancing as a whole?
· Presence and Poise: Is the group performing as if they know the dances?
· Costume: Are the costumes authentic? Do the costumes add to the performance?
· Theme: Is the theme chosen for the topikos round clearly depicted by the dance?
· Props: Are the props used relevant to the theme?
· Authenticity: Is the dance truly from the original round category?
