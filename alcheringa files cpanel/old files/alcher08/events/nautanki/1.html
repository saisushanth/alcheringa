Nautanki – Radio Show

When we go Retro… we go the whole hog! Yes, we are back to the good ol’ days, when your life used to revolve around the radio. It’s all very well expressing yourselves on stage when everyone can see you, but can you get your voice to do the acting.

This is the Nautanki of the voice. Let’s see if your imagination has got what it takes to paint a picture with your mouth.

And you will be the star of Alcher ’08 with your voice pervading our campus-wide radio, the Radio-‘G’.
