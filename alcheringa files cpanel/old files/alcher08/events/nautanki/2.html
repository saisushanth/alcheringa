Nautanki – Radio Show

This is the Nautanki of the voice. You will be the star of Alcher ’08 with your voice pervading our campus-wide radio, the Radio-‘G’.

  1. The team is supposed to present a radio show.
  2. The recording will be done live at Alcheringa.
  3. Participants per team should be 2

ROUND 1 AWAAZ HI ANDAAZ

  1. Time allotted per team is 12 minutes with distribution as:
    • Prepared content : 9 minutes
    • 3 Songs of 1 minute each : 3 minutes
  2. Prepared content has to be prepared beforehand. It can be anything which you think will entertain the listener.
  3. The 3 songs have to be chosen by the teams themselves and have to be brought in a CD in mp3 format and will be edited during the recording.
  4. The teams have to carry their underlay and sound effects(refer ***)

ROUND 2 BOLTI BANDH (FOR THE 3 TEAMS SELECTED FROM ROUND 1)

  1. Team will be given a topic on-the-spot.
  2. Time for preparation is 2 minutes.
  3. Performance time is 2 minutes.

JUDGING CRITERIA

  • Hook Presell* :15%
  • Content :20%
  • Voice Quality :15%
  • Presentation* * :10%
  • Coordination :10%
  • Spontaneity :20%
  • Underlay and sound effects***:10%

***Underlay:background music. Sound effects:if u r using some sound bytes like the barking of a dog etc...
** Hook presell: A story that you formulate supported by the songs to be played. *Presentation: Voice modulation, fluency, mood.
