Wordsworth – Literary Challenge

Fasten your seatbelts… Here comes Wordsworth with nail biting literary competitions.

For the first time in Alcheringa, we have Scrabble! Your very own board
game going hi-tech, with intriguing clues, exciting words,
on-screen display and surely the same old charm.

Turncoat is a challenge to show your skills in equivocation.
Speak in favour of the topic for half the time and against for the rest and
leave the audience spellbound with your display of self-contradiction.

The most interesting of all, Wordsworth quiz, with rounds
including Anagrams, Dumbcharades, Audio/visuals, and much more...

Impromptu- the Extempore. Be spontaneous! Score more for being as glib and cogent as possible.

Time for some 'Reel' writing. Let your creative instincts flow,
taking reference from a short movie and using your own ideas.
