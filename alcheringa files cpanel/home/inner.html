GENERAL GUIDELINES
1. The teams have to be punctual. Any team that’s not ready by the specified time may be disqualified.
2. If the lighting facility is to be availed, one person of the group is permitted to supervise the lighting during the performance. Otherwise it would be taken care of by the Lights and Sounds group of the organizing team. Creativity and innovation will fetch extra points.

6. The name of the submitted audio file should be same as the name of the team.
7. Marks will be deducted for exceeding the time limit. One member from the team must sit with the sound technician and the computer operator.
8. Clearing of the stage within the maximum time limit is the responsibility of the team.

10.Vulgarity of any form will not be allowed in the competition. So, if a team feels that any stunt could be termed as vulgar, it would be best to speak of it to the respective event organizer, at least an hour before performing it on the stage.
